About

rCAT is a web-based tool for the characterization of characters and analysis of relations between them in literature. The output of the tool is a PDF file that includes network graph of characters, graph details, word clouds, as well as time-bound development of specific vocabulary.
The tool was developed by Florian Barth, Evgeny Kim, Roman Klinger, and Sandra Murr with the support of CRETA project funded by Federal Ministry for Education and Research (Germany).
